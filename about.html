The Pe-mic Story

Pe-Mic Consulting was founded by Babalola Ademola Michael having under gone tutelage in ITB Nig Limited a leading construction company in Nigeria which is based in Lagos.

Although it was started with an Architectural design and little constructions projects, today Pe-Mic Consulting is growing fast in every aspect in order to meet the demand of the fast growing client based and new industry challenges. We are supported by a strong management team comprising people with relevant technical experience.

We at Pe-Mic Consulting believes that a conducive environment is based on a functional, and satisfactory space for the productivity and the quality of surrounding of users; we strive to provide excellent services to satisfy the challenging need of our client.
